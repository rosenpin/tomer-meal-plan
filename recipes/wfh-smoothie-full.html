WFH first choice green smoothie with vegetables

Ingredients

  • 300ml soy drink
  • 30g pea protein powder
  • 50g spinach or kale (big handful)
  • 50g beets, raw
  • 1 medium banana (120g)
  • 5g fresh ginger root
  • 7g honey (1 teaspoon)

Instructions

  1. Add soy drink and pea protein to blender
  2. Add spinach/kale, beets, banana, and ginger
  3. Add honey
  4. Blend until smooth
