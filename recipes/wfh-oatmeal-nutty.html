Ingredients

  • 60g quick oats
  • 15g cashews, raw
  • 15g walnuts
  • 10g chia seeds
  • 7g honey (1 teaspoon)
  • 200ml soy drink
  • 1 medium banana (120g)

Instructions

  1. Cook oats with soy drink
  2. Stir in chia seeds
  3. Top with cashews, walnuts, and sliced banana
  4. Drizzle with honey
