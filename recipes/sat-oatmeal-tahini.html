Ingredients

  • 70g quick oats
  • 30g tahini (2 tablespoons)
  • 10g flax seeds
  • 7g honey (drizzle)
  • 1 medium banana (120g)
  • 200ml soy drink
  • 200ml orange juice, fresh

Instructions

  1. Cook oats with soy drink
  2. Stir in flax seeds and tahini
  3. Top with sliced banana and honey
  4. Serve with orange juice
