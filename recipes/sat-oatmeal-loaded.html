Ingredients

  • 70g quick oats
  • 10g chia seeds
  • 10g flax seeds
  • 15g cashews, raw
  • 15g walnuts
  • 48g Medjool dates (2 dates)
  • 16g natural peanut butter (1 tablespoon)
  • 200ml soy drink
  • 200ml orange juice, fresh

Instructions

  1. Cook oats with soy drink
  2. Stir in chia and flax seeds
  3. Top with cashews, walnuts, chopped dates, and peanut butter
  4. Serve with a glass of orange juice
