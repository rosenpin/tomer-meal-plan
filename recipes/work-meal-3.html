Work Lunch Plate

Sunday–Tuesday lunch at work

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast, grilled
  • 200g quinoa or white rice, cooked
  • 90g cucumber, chopped
  • 90g bell pepper, chopped
  • 90g tomato, chopped
  • 30g lettuce
  • 20g carrot, shaved
  • 1 tablespoon corn kernels
  • 2 tablespoons mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons spring onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 100g sweet potato, baked (if available)

Instructions

  1. Plate grilled chicken breast with quinoa or rice
  2. Prepare salad bowl on the side
  3. Drizzle olive oil over salad
  4. Add sweet potato if available
