WFH first choice oatmeal bowl with seeds and tahini

Ingredients

  • 60g quick oats
  • 10g chia seeds
  • 10g flax seeds
  • 15g tahini (1 tablespoon)
  • 7g honey (1 teaspoon)
  • 200ml soy drink
  • 48g Medjool dates (2 dates)

Instructions

  1. Cook oats with soy drink
  2. Stir in chia seeds and flax seeds
  3. Top with tahini and chopped dates
  4. Drizzle with honey
