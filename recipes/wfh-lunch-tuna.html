Ingredients

  • 130g tuna, canned in water, drained (1 can)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 200g white rice, cooked
  • 150g broccoli or cauliflower, cooked
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook rice and steam vegetables
  2. Fry or scramble eggs
  3. Plate tuna, eggs, rice, and vegetables
  4. Drizzle with olive oil
  5. Note: limit tuna to maximum 2 times per week
