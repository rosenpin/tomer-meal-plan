Work Afternoon Oatmeal

Sunday–Tuesday afternoon oatmeal bowl at work

Ingredients

  • 60g quick oats
  • 30g protein granola
  • 16g natural peanut butter (1 tablespoon)
  • 15g tahini (1 tablespoon)
  • 7g honey (drizzle)
  • 200ml soy drink

Instructions

  1. Combine oats with soy drink
  2. Top with granola, peanut butter, and tahini
  3. Drizzle with honey
