WFH second choice lighter smoothie with fruit on the side

Ingredients

  • 300ml soy drink
  • 30g pea protein powder
  • 1 medium banana (120g)
  • 20g spinach or kale (small handful)
  • 1 medium kiwi (75g)
  • 1 clementine (74g)

Instructions

  1. Blend soy drink, pea protein, banana, and greens
  2. Serve kiwi and clementine on the side
