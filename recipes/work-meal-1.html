Work Morning Bowl

Sunday–Tuesday morning meal at work

Ingredients

  • 250g cottage cheese (3% fat)
  • 1 large egg
  • 100g avocado
  • 90g cucumber, chopped
  • 90g bell pepper, chopped
  • 90g tomato, chopped
  • 30g lettuce
  • 20g carrot, shaved
  • 1 tablespoon corn kernels
  • 2 tablespoons mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons spring onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 200ml orange juice, fresh

Instructions

  1. Prepare salad bowl with cucumber, bell pepper, tomato, lettuce, carrot shavings, corn, mushrooms, and spring onion
  2. Add cottage cheese, egg (boiled or fried), and sliced avocado
  3. Drizzle with olive oil
  4. Serve with a glass of fresh orange juice
