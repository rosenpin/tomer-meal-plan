Ingredients

  • 70g quick oats

  • 7g honey (drizzle)
  • 1 medium banana (120g)
  • 200ml soy drink
  • 200ml orange juice, fresh

  2. Top with peanut butter, sliced banana, and honey
  3. Serve with kiwi and orange juice
